Chocolate Cake Recipe

Home Recipes Contact

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, melted butter, and milk. Stir well.
  4. Pour into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes until done.
  6. Cool and serve.
